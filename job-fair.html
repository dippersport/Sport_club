Technology Career Network

Computer Science Job Fair

TCN's Computer Science Job Fair, hosted at least once a year, is where club members can be exposed to some of the best local companies in the tech industry in Lancaster.

Attendees will be able to network with industry professionals and discover exciting career opportunities. With a wide variety of job opportunities available for those that attend, the Job Fair is sure to provide an engaging and enriching experience to all attendees, making it an ideal platform for those looking to grow and develop their career in tech.
