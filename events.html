TCN Events and Meetings

The TCN hosts monthly meetings with guest speakers, Q&A sessions, refreshments and more for networking and socializing with other members.

Meetings with guest speakers are typically hosted on the last Friday of the month during Fall and Spring semesters (depending on guest availability). During the meetings, we usually provide pizza, snacks, and drinks as refreshments for our club members. In between the larger get-togethers, we host small events such as resume building workshops.

Meetings Archive

If you were not able to attend a previous TCN meeting or if you are curious to see what TCN meetings are like, below is an archive of our recorded meetings.

[03/22/2023] How to Go From CS Student to a Job

For students who are wondering the steps from finishing school to landing their first job, TCN member Andrew Foster covers it all - from how to write your best resume to what to say during an interview!

[10/27/2022] Julie Gardner - WebFX

Julie Gardner is a Talent Acquisition Specialist from WebFX, a digital marketing agency and web/software development company with many locations, including Harrisburg, PA. She shared an overview of the company, and answered questions about the recruitment process.

[3/25/2022] Chris Carney - Crafting a Great Resume

Chris Carney is a Software Development Engineer at Amazon and a professional resume critic. During this Zoom meeting, Chris shared with us a wealth of tips / insider knowledge on making a resume perfect for industry, such as formatting, do's & don'ts, what hiring managers are looking for, and more.

[03/04/2022] Richard Abel and Hallie Lupinski - Deloitte

Deloitte is an international service provider of financial advisory, consulting, auditing, and more. In this Zoom meeting, recruiters Richard Abel and Hallie Lupinski gave us an overview of what to look forward to in an interview, industry trends, and held a Q&A for students.

[2/18/2022] Guest Speaker: Lea Eller

Lea Eller is a Cyber Defense Infrastructure Engineer from Penn State Health & College of Medicine, and a Millersville alumni. In her presentation, she spoke about her journey through her career in the rapidly growing field of Cyber Security and gave career advice for prospecting students. Fun fact: Lea was also the founder and first president of the Cyber Defense Organization (CDO) at Millersville University!
