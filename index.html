TECHNOLOGY CAREER NETWORK

The TCN at Millersville is a club for professional development, networking, and shared resources. 

We host events geared to support career development of CS & STEM majors at Millersville. With guest speakers, networking, and workshops such as resume-building and mock interviews, the TCN is your opportunity to jumpstart your Technology Career!
